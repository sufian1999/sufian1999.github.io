How it works

One rule. Endless runs.

STEP 01

Swipe to merge

Slide every tile in one direction. Two equal numbers that collide combine into one that's double.

STEP 02

Hit the target

Reach the 2048 tile — or 8192 on 6×6 Hard — to clear the board's goal and bank the win.

STEP 03

Keep going

Push past the target for a higher score, then take your best to the daily leaderboard.
